# Recommended Permissions for Simple Employee

This document outlines the recommended permissions to assign to a standard employee. These permissions are focused on self-service functionalities and viewing relevant personal and company information. By default, all employees with attendance configured can view the attendance table as well as create leave requests.

## Biometric

-   **`Create Request`**: Allows employees to create their own biometric requests.

## Holiday

-   **`View`**: Allows employees to view company holidays.

## Notice Board

-   **`View`**: Allows employees to view company notices.

## Payroll

-   **`View Records`**: Allows employees to view their individual payroll records (salary slips).
